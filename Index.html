OUR SERVICES

BOOK MEASUREMENT

Schedule your desired service with our convinient online booking system. You can choose a time that fits your schedule from the comfort of your home or on the go. Simply select your desired service, pick a date and time, and confirm your appointment in just a few clicks.

HOME PICKUP

We provide doorstep services for your stitching needs and we send our consultants (Tailor) to your home or office. They help you with choosing new Designs for your clothes, taking perfect measurements and collecting fabric from you.

STICHING AND FAST DELIVERY

Post which the stitching happens at our Production House and then we get it delivered to you and if there will be any alteration required, We will redo again and redeliver it at your place.

What we stitch for men?

Premium stitching for men is available from us, and it will be delivered straight to your doorstep, or home and office. We have specialists on hand that have a wealth of knowledge and expertise in this field from the last 38 years, which makes it tough to find a competent men’s tailor in today’s market.

What we stitch for woman?

For all sorts of women’s dresses like blouses, suits, and lehengas, whether they are for regular office wear, special events, or party apparel, we provide free pick-up and delivery services to our customers’ doorsteps. The fact that you will not be required to monitor your stitching orders or visit physical locations in order to complete them means that conducting business online is very safe and trustworthy.

What we stitch for children?

Because the task of picking an outfit for your child might be onerous. To make their lives simpler, we provide personalised doorstep services for your kids, no matter whether they are girls or boys.
